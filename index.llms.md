# Lab Manual

This website serves as the lab manual for the LMU Open Science Center.

## For Management

- For templates and examples of **responses to inquiries** frequently received by the OSC, see the [Responding to Requests](osc-outreach/responding-requests.llms.md).

## For Staff

- For general **administration** information and protocols, go to the [Administration](staff-onboarding/administration.llms.md) section.

- For templates and examples of **responses to inquiries** frequently received by the OSC, see the [Responding to Requests](osc-outreach/responding-requests.llms.md).

## For Hiwis

Different Hiwis support different OSC activities. Use the sections below to quickly find the parts of the manual most relevant to your work.

- If you are involved in **public outreach, social media management, newsletter preparation, or content creation**, see the [Public Outreach](osc-outreach/index.llms.md) chapter.

- If you are working on the **Train-the-Trainer** team, see the [Train-the-Trainer](path/link.qmd) chapter.

- If you are assisting with the planning and organization of OSC events, see the [OSC Events](osc-events/index.llms.md) chapter.

- If you are responsible for updating or maintaining the OSC website, see the [Updating the Website](updating-website/index.llms.md) chapter.

## For Technical Hiwis

- If you are working closely with the **technical team**, checkout the [Technical Overview](tech-overview.llms.md) for detailed information on the technologies and platforms used at the OSC.

- For detailed information on **navigating GitHub** and its uses, see the sections within the [GitHub](github/index.llms.md) chapter.

> **TIP:**
>
> If you are new to the OSC, we recommend first reading the [Working Environment](staff-onboarding/working-envi.llms.md) and [Technology Overview](tech-overview.llms.md) chapters to become familiar with the tools and workflows used at the OSC.
